My Animations

Below are my animations, same as I put on youtube as well as the links to them, but hoping to put more details and descriptions in here. The details will more go into where I was in my blender journey creating it and notes on what I think went wrong, right, or how I might do things differently in the future.

The dreams are numbered in the order I have them, but I create and upload them in the order of how difficult I think they would be to make it with my current skillset. Newest on top, oldest on bottom.

As always, no AI (or at least none I'm aware of) is used in the making of any of these creative endeavors. All hand made by yours truely, where my skills, limitations, and best attempts are all on display to capture my dreams.

Dream 7 - Eclipse

Upload Date: September 11, 2025

Youtube Link

Notes:

This one I was really excited for. I had my powerful laptop and more blender tutorials under my belt. I know it is a black hole and not an eclipse, that's just how my dream self identified it. I completely forgot that there were wood textures and normal maps available and I made everything whole cloth. I could have probably been a little smarter about the dirt and tried not to make the spacing of the wood in the cabin quite so uniform and perfect, but I think it gives it a little more of that dream quality.

I forgot how to change the sounds volumes in the blender video editor, and the accretion disk is rotating way too fast, but it gives it that home-made charm.

Dream 4 - Camper Van

Upload Date: October 16, 2024

Youtube Link

Notes:

This was another one I struggled with. The trees created by a blender add on were much too difficult for my 8 year old laptop to work with. It was like going back to the windows movie maker days where I loved adding a lot of sound effects and details to my videos, but it always bloated to the point where I would have to save after every action because it always crashed on that second one.

I realized if I was going to go further with this I needed something beefier. I splurged on a gaming laptop (I like the portability, I can't help it) after finishing this render.

The van body and windshield gave me way more trouble than they should have. I was definitely doing it wrong, but kept fighting through it because I wanted to get it done. The grass was also supposed to blow and be more computer generated, but I had trouble finding tutorials online, so i used the geometry node method, as slow and painful as it was. The leaves were shaking way harder than I thought they would be. But since the render took like 5+ days to go through, I wasn't going to do it again.'

Dream 0 - Aquarium

Upload Date: September 2, 2024

Youtube Link

Notes:

This was the first dream in a whole set of them that were remarkably different than anything I had before. I had this the end of summer 2018 (best guess). I was known for having wild dreams before this, but this one was especially unique.

I had just come off the donut tutorial and jumped into making this one. A lot of the models are rough, and my struggles inspired me to go a character modelling tutorial later.

From what the person in the dream says, that in part defined the whole theme of these dreams going forward, and, despite my limitations, I thought it would be best to capture it right out the gate. And as you can see, my dream-self was not happy with the news.

It still took me a few years to fully admit who I am to myself and why it is numbered 0. Forced to self reflect during the 2020 lockdowns.
